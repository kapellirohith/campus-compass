# Freshers Survival Sheet
**Campus Compass | Amrita Amaravati**

---

### 1. The FA Rule (Attendance)
**Do not drop below 75%.** If you do, you receive an FA (Failure by Attendance) and cannot write the final exam. Attendance is marked hourly. Check AUMS every Friday.

### 2. The Digital Arsenal
*   **AUMS:** Academic portal (grades, attendance, fees).
*   **GPMS:** Gate Pass Management System. You literally cannot leave campus without an approved pass.
*   **GitHub Student Pack:** Claim it on Day 1 for free tools.

### 3. Packing Checklist (Crucial Items)
*   Aadhar Card (Original + 5 Copies)
*   10th & 12th Marksheets (Originals)
*   15 Passport Size Photos
*   Laptop + Surge Protector Extension Board
*   Formals (Strictly enforced for classes)

### 4. Club Advice
Join early (Month 1). You do not need to be an expert to join Chakravyuha, bi0s, or IEEE. You join to learn. If you wait until you feel "ready," you will be a spectator while others organize events.

### 5. Emergency Reminders
*   Save the Warden's and Class Advisor's numbers on Day 1.
*   Never try to proxy attendance. The Disciplinary Committee checks.
*   Back up your laptop regularly before mid-terms.
